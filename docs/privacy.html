Privacy Policy

Gym App · Effective: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gym App ("we", "us", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to run the app and the features you choose to use:

  • Account information. When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple-provided user identifier and, if you allow it, your name and email address.
  • Profile information. Information you provide in your profile, including username, display name, and an optional profile photo.
  • Workout data. Workouts, exercises, sets, modules, sessions, notes, and other fitness data you record.
  • Social content. Posts, comments, reactions, friendships, direct messages, content reports, and related moderation information that you create within the app.
  • Device and notification tokens. A Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver push notifications, and basic device information needed to deliver and troubleshoot them.
  • Diagnostic information. Crash reports and error logs collected through Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.

We do not collect advertising identifiers, we do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not sell your personal information.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To sync your workouts and other content between your devices.
  • To enable social features you choose to use (friends, posts, messages), enforce our Terms, and respond to content reports.
  • To send push notifications you have opted into.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability.

3. How We Store and Share Information

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Messaging, and Crashlytics). Google processes this data as our service provider under their own privacy and security commitments.

We share information only:

  • With other users when you intentionally share it (for example, sending a message or posting to your feed).
  • With service providers (Firebase) strictly to run the app.
  • When required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of users or the public.

4. Your Choices

  • Access your data. Your data is visible in the app at any time. You can export it from Settings → Export Data.
  • Delete your account. You can permanently delete your account, cloud data, and local data on your device from Settings → Delete Account. Some content shared with other users (e.g. messages they have received) may persist in their copies.
  • Push notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Camera and photos. The app requests access to your camera and photo library only when you choose to set a profile photo.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information and content for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your cloud-stored data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard practices — including HTTPS transport encryption and Firebase's at-rest encryption — to protect your information. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Users

By using the app, you understand that your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version at the same URL and update the "Effective" date above. Material changes will be communicated within the app.
